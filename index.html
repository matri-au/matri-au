Theory

Human ecology is the science of applied Yoga practice.

Ecology is applied awareness. It is the observation of consequence — how thought produces reaction and re-enactment shapes environment.

Traditional cultures preserve this through restraint, continuity, and relationship to land.

Vitality is the outcome of self-regulation. Clean inputs. Steady rhythm. Trained attention.

Restored physiology restores clarity, memory and stamina. Restored clarity restores right action.

Everything is bound by the cycle of transmutation.

Raja Yoga produces positive transformation building a solid foundation.

Discipline -> Order -> Space -> Clarity -> Relaxation -> Force

Alignment is not belief. It is measurable coherence between thought, action, and environment.
