Theory

Human ecology is applied Yoga.

Ecology is applied awareness.
It observes consequence.
Thought produces reaction.
Repetition shapes environment.

Traditional cultures preserve this through restraint, continuity, and relationship to land.

Vitality emerges from regulation.
Clean inputs.
Steady rhythm.
Trained attention.

Restored physiology restores clarity, memory and stamina. Restored clarity restores right action.

Everything is under a spell of transmutation.

Raja Yoga stabilises the changing.

Effort -> Discipline -> Order -> Space -> Clarity -> Relaxation -> Force

Alignment is not belief.
It is measurable coherence between thought, action, and environment.
