Orientation

Matri Raja Yoga is a corrective field of continuous observation.

It does not negotiate with distraction. It returns attention to the body, to the breath, to right action — and to consequences.

Ecology is lived law. Indigenous and native cultures carry it through restraint, custom, and relationship to land.

Matri treats culture as custody: a duty to preserve what keeps people human.

Vitality is regulation. Clean inputs, steady rhythm, trained attention.

Detox and committed practice restore stamina, clarity, memory, and calm force.

Guidance

MATRI Raja Yoga is a sofisticated self-sufficient integral system.

It promotes eco-yoga culture, and empowers community — improving lives without noise and pressures.

Common problems: depleted energy, scattered mind, anxiety, poor memory, lost motivation.

Solution method: right effort → detox → rhythm → attention → right action.

Work-frame: Hatha · Karma · Jnana · Bhakti Yogas.

The MATRI Raja Yoga goal: a bridge between the Western ecology & Eastern indigenous "head-heart-hands" (HHH) timeless self-development frameworks.

This path is open for all who who walk the walk, and commit.
