Matri — Raja yoga instruction. Training attention as sacred custody. Fierce and motherly soft presence.

Invitation

Ecology is lived law.

Not a cause. Not a stance.
Restraint practiced. Custom honoured. Land tended.

This work meets you in the pause between breaths—
where space expands and time dissolves.

Here, softness holds you completely.
And fierceness refuses to let you hide.

You will not be comforted.
You will be met.

The Work

For those feeling:

scattered · depleted · compressed by noise · severed from ground

The path:

right effort → detox → rhythm → trained attention → right action

Hatha for the body.
Karma for the will.
Jñāna for clarity.
Bhakti for the heart.

A whole system.
Not observed. Walked.

Lineage

The lineage moves through silence.

Himalayan roots. Unbroken thread.
Not performers. Practitioners.

I do not teach from myself.
I hold space for the practice to move through.
This is the only way it arrives intact.
